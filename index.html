About Me

Arizona is basically all I know, I was born here and have lived here for 26 years. Unfortunately, I hate the heat, I have my whole life, but my family lives in Phoenix, so I endure. My goal is to move somewhere with actual seasons in the next few years. I really want to go to South America and Europe someday, and just go to as many places as I can, and enjoy as many different cultures as I can.

I have been playing music since I could remember. Music has always been very influential for me. Unfortunately, unless you are in a mainstream band, the music performance industry does not pay the bills. Fast track to today, I am now studying to be a developer, and I am super excited to learn as much as I can.
